Save The Animals

Key Conservation is helping conservationist to receive critical funding and increased global support through an app that provides real-time updates on day-to-day campaings. We can stop global extinction if we utilize new technology and interact with supporters in many new ways!

Supporters

As a supporter you have the ability to view the details of a compaign which include urgency level, funding goal of the campaign and info on the conservationist that posted the campaign

Orginizers

Will have the ability to post new campaigns with funding goals and let supporters know the urgency level and give feedback on how they are helping to save the animals
